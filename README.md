The Auto Supply Point-of-Sale Management System provides essential functionalities that help store owners efficiently manage automotive parts and supplier information. Its key features include adding, viewing, updating, and deleting product records, allowing users to easily maintain accurate and organized inventory data. The system also enables users to modify stock quantities and prices, track product availability, and record important supplier details for better coordination and sourcing.

Another important functionality of the system is its ability to generate alerts for low-stock items, helping store owners monitor inventory levels and prevent shortages.
